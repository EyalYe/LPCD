# 02 Presentation and Video (30%)

## Objective
Teach the class about near/sub-threshold design and provide a high-level video summary.
4 speakers, each fully owns their section — slides, speaker notes, and Kahoot questions.

## Deliverables
- **Class Presentation (20%)**: 4 separate speaker files + shared video script
- **Short Video Summary (10%)**: [Video Script](video_script.md) — 4 sections, one per speaker

---

## File Format (unified)

Each speaker now has **two companion files** in a consistent format:

- **Slide-info file** — `speaker_N_<topic>(Name).md`: per-slide `**On screen**` + `**Speaker notes**`, plus Kahoot questions, visuals, and a coordination note.
- **Script file** — `speaker_N_script.md`: the **verbatim spoken narration**, one block per slide, matching the slide titles/timings of the companion slide-info file.

The original raw drafts (`intro.md`, `Omri - MEP.md`, `Eli - ADVC`) are preserved in [`source_drafts/`](source_drafts/) for reference.
