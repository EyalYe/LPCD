# LPCD: Low Power Circuit Design (Nanoscale Era)

## Project Topic: Operating Circuits at Extreme Low Voltages
**Mission:** Achieving Maximum Energy Efficiency through Sub-Threshold and Near-Threshold Computing in the 14Å process era.

---

## 🎯 Overview
This project explores the fundamental physics and implementation challenges of operating integrated circuits where the supply voltage ($V_{DD}$) is lower than or near the threshold voltage ($V_{th}$). While this "Sub-Threshold" regime offers exponential power savings, it introduces significant hurdles in speed, reliability, and variability.

Our goal is to identify the **Minimum Energy Point (MEP)** and propose a novel method to optimize the current state-of-the-art in ultra-low-power nanoscale VLSI design.
